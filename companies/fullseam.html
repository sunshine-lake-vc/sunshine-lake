Fullseam

As a systems integrator in Toronto, Maya spent years stitching together ERPs that were never designed to talk to each other. She decided to build the middleware that should have existed all along — and Fullseam was born.

FullSeam is an AI employee for finance teams that logs into a company’s financial stack and executes routine workflows like invoicing, reconciliation, and collections.
