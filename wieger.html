Wieger Lamers

EMEA & Platforms Lead

Wieger is an investor at Sunshine Lake, based in London. He grew up outside Amsterdam in an entrepreneurial family, with a front-row seat to Adyen's journey from a 12-person team to one of Europe's defining IPOs.

Before Sunshine Lake, he worked in venture at Valia Ventures and in early-stage operations at Unlock (Antler), which was later acquired. During his studies, he interviewed 300+ immigrant founders globally, exploring the link between early adversity and entrepreneurial success.

He holds a BSc in Business (Finance) from ESCP, and in his spare time follows Dutch history and AFC Ajax.
